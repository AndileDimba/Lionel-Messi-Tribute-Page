Lionel Messi

The Man who Conqured Football

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina.

  • 2000 - He signed a professional contract with FC Barcelona.

  • 2001 - He played for junior team and scored a number of goals.

  • 2004/2005 - He made his competitive debut against Espanyol and scored his first goal Albacete at Camp Nou. Won Laligawith FC Barcelona and Under-20 FIFA World Cup.

  • 2005/2006 - He won his first Champions League/ Spanish Super Cup/ Laliga Santader Title with FC Barcelona.

  • 2008/2009 - He won his Champions League/Spanish Cup/Laliga Title with FC Barcelona, He also was a top goal scorer in Champions League/ Copa del Rey. Won Best Fifa Men's player/ Ballon D'OR/Player of the Year.

  • 2010 - He won his second Ballon D'OR/ Best FIFA men's player/ FIFA Club World Cup, Copa del Rey, Champions League top scorer.

  • 2011 - He won his third Ballon D'OR/ Best FIFA men's player/ Laliga's top goal scorer.

  • 2012 - He won his fourth Ballon D'OR and scored 91 goals in a calendar year with Laliga Santader.

  • 2015 - He won his fifth Ballon D'OR with a treble of Champions League Laliga Santader/ Copa del Rey.

  • 2019 - He won his sixth Ballon D'OR with Laliga Santader and He is FC Barcelona's Captain.

  • 2020 - Currently is the FC Barcelona's Captain and Top goal scorer of the club and Laliga Santader. He currently has 699 goals under his belt.
